About Us

Mainland Infracon is a Limited Liability Partnership firm promoted by professionals with a combined experience of more than 100 years. It is promoted by professionals with qualifications in Civil Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Construction Management and Business Management.


Established in 2017, Mainland Infracon is presently developing more than 1,775,000 sq ft real estate and plans to start projects of another 500,000 sq ft development in short time.


Mainland is based out of Ahmedabad, the commercial capital of Gujarat, India. It is formed with an objective of developing real estate in and around Ahmedabad and involve itself in Technology Transfer in the domain of construction and urban infrastructure.


Established in 2017, Mainland Infracon is presently developing more than 1,575,000 sq ft real estate and plans to start projects of another 500,000 sq ft development in short time.

Managment

Praful Dave

With more than 40 years of experience in construction and real estate development, Praful Dave, a Civil Engineer by qualification, oversees all projects in their evaluation stage. Praful Dave has executed more than 50 real estate projects in Gujarat and continues to be active in Mainland in his capacity of an Executive Consultant.

Naresh Thakar

Naresh Thakar a designated partner in Mainland retired from a senior position in Government of Gujarat. A qualified Civil Engineer and an expert on Environment, he has vast experience in handling technologies that protect the environment. With over 40 years of experience in the field he plays an advisory role at Mainland.

Mihir Maniar

Mihir Maniar is a partner at Mainland with more than 18 years of experience in Real Estate development. He has experience in handling construction technologies as well as urban infrastructure in advisory as well as executive capacity. Holding a Masters in Construction Management from CEPT, he is a joint promoter at Mainland and presently the technology and engineering head for all projects being executed at Mainland.

Bhaumik Thakar

Bhaumik Thakar heads partner relations, investments, business development and new businesses at Mainland. With more than 15 years of experience of working in Government sector, he handles regulatory and legal aspects of businesses of Mainland. A graduate of the St Xavier’s College, he focusses on market expansion and front-ending in pre-execution stages of projects.
